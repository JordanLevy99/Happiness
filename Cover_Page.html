Finding the Key to Happiness

As of 2019, Finland is the happiest country in the world. In fact, Finland has been the happiest country for two years in a row, according to the World Happiness Report. As a brief background, the World Happiness Report is an annual survey on the global state of happiness. The World Happiness Report also measures the extent of how much GDP, freedom, social support(meaning friends and family), lack of government corruption, generosity and life expectancy has contributed to the reported levels of happiness. In the last five years, the World Happiness Report have found that Nordic countries have consitently taken the titles for top 5 happiest countries in the world. In comparison, the United States is ranked as the 19th happiest country in 2019 and every year, our happiness rank falls lower than the year before. The United States likes to pride itself as one of the richest countries in the world and one of the freest countries in the world, but data shows that our people are getting more and more unhappy every year. So what makes Nordic countries so much happier than the rest of us? What is the secret to happiness and can we use it to improve on our own lives?
